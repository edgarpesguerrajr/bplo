MAKE MODEL MOTOR NO. CHASSIS NO. LTO PLATE NO. MTOP PLATE NO. TODA NO.
{{ make }}{{ year_model }}{{ motor_no }}{{ chassis_no }} {{ plate_no }}{{ mtop_plate }}

Validity: This Motorized Tricycle Operator’s Permit (MTOP) shall be valid for a period of two (2) years.

MTOP not renewed after two (2) years from expiry date shall be deemed cancelled/revoked (per Section 94 and 101 of Municipal Ordinance No. 22, S-2019)

Subject to the following terms and conditions per Sections 96 (c) and 97 of Municipal Ordinance No. 22, S-2019:

The MTOP grantee shall

  1. Provide the unit with trash can;
  2. Carry a common carrier’s insurance sufficient to answer for any liability it may incur to passengers and third parties in case of accidents; employ drivers duly licensed by the Land Transportation Office (LTO), who at all times wear the prescribed ID for proper identification;
  3. Report in writing to the Board, if they intend to stop service completely, or suspend service for more than one (1) month;
  4. Comply with the Number Coding Scheme and prescribed mudguard size (1 foot x 2 feet), and place of display;
  5. Collect only the fare rate allowed by applicable local ordinances, rules and regulations;
  6. Register the tricycle unit with the LTO Field Office with jurisdiction over the Municipality of Tanay;
  7. Comply with existing laws, ordinances, policies, rules and regulations for tricycle-for-hire services as well as with the terms and conditions stated herein. Non-compliance therewith shall be sufficient cause for the suspension or cancellation/revocation of this Provisional Authority.

Prohibited Acts per Section 99 of Municipal Ordinance No. 22, S-2019. The following acts are strictly prohibited:

  1. No tricycle operator and/or driver shall operate within the Municipality without the required Motorized Tricycle Operator’s Permit (MTOP).
  2. No tricycle operator/driver shall carry more than three (3) passengers, likewise he shall be held liable for overloading.
  3. No tricycle operator/driver shall be allowed to operate his unit if he is under the influence of intoxicating beverages or prohibited drugs.
  4. No tricycles shall operate on national highways utilized by 4 wheel vehicles greater than 4 tons and where normal speed exceeds 40 KPH.
